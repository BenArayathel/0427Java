Lucid Dreaming

Dreaming while consciously aware you're dreaming

One simple way to get started lucid dreaming is to ask yourself as often as possible if you're dreaming or not. Doing this regularly throughout your waking life will eventually inspire you to ask this question while dreaming.

Asking is typically not enough to know if you are dreaming or not. You're better than you might think at rationalizing your dreams as perfectly normal and not out of the ordinary at all. You must check. There are a few characteristics of dreams that you can take advantage of to test if you are awake or dreaming.

# Technique Awake Dreaming
1 Close mouth, hold nose closed, and use nose to breath Can't breath Air flows normally (unless nose is blocked in real life)
2 Look at symbols, such as books, signs, digial clocks, then look away and look back symbols will be the same the second time as they were the first time symbols will be different the second time from the first time, or they won't make sense at all either time
3 Look at hands Hands look as expected Hands look out of proportion, different color, or possibly more or less fingers
