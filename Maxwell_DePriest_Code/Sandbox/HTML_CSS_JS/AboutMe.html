About Me:


I live in Columbia, SC. I recently moved here from Austin, TX. I enjoy playing videogames, bar trivia, and disc golf. I'm a big cinephile so that's my expertise when we play trivia. My favorite game of all time is the Witcher 3. I have a cat. He was only born with one eye so I named him Willie after the pirate from the Goonies. I like to read as well. I'm currently re-reading the Lord of the Rings trilogy. I grew up in Pittsburgh so my sports teams all wear black and gold. I'm primarily just a Penguins fan, but I'll root for the Steelers or Pirates if the game is on. I've got a bit of a twisted sense of humor. My favorite web comic is Cyanide and Happiness
